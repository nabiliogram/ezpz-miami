Outdoor Play 4 spots
Morningside Park
Upper East Side · 0.4 mi
Splash pad, proper playground, and actual shade — the holy trinity. Tennis courts have a backboard for solo practice. Parking is rarely a problem on weekday mornings.
FreeAll ages
Oleta River State Park
North Miami Beach · 3.4 mi
Florida's largest urban park. Kayak rentals, mountain bike trails, and a beach that doesn't require driving to the coast. The mangrove tunnels by kayak are worth it.
$6 / vehicle3+
Greynolds Park
North Miami Beach · 4.1 mi
Less known than Oleta but worth it. A small lake, paddleboat rentals, and one of the few Miami parks with actual rolling terrain. Picnic shelters book out on weekends.
FreeAll ages
Arch Creek Park
North Miami · 2.8 mi
A natural limestone arch in the middle of Miami. The small nature center has live snakes and turtles. Good for a short focused outing rather than an all-day visit.
Free4+
